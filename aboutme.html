Hi, I'm Marco. With over 15 years of experience in visual arts, I have honed my skills as a photographer, image editor, and videographer, collaborating with numerous companies, agencies, and magazines. My passion for working alongside Art Directors and Designers has fueled my drive to bring visual ideas to life.

Now, I'm channeling my extensive graphical and visual expertise into the world of frontend development. Combining my creative background with newly acquired coding skills, I am dedicated to finding innovative solutions for implementing stunning visual designs.

In addition to photography, movies have always been a great passion of mine. They encompass everything I love: stunning visuals, technical achievements, storytelling, psychology, and the human experience. Another passion of mine is video games, especially board games. The strategy, planning, and problem-solving in board games are akin to the challenges in programming, which I find incredibly stimulating. I'm also a huge backgammon enthusiast, captivated by the blend of strategy and the tactical aspect of constantly facing new challenges.
